# Brand fingerprint

- Story: a private Highland itinerary begins with the visitor's interests rather than a pre-set list of stops.
- Visual fingerprint: cinematic blue landscape, cream paper, rust accents, Georgia travel-journal typography and an asymmetrical image collage using the official mark.
- Section order: emotional invitation → interest-based choice → what to send the team → factual contact hand-off.
- Conversion path: choose landscape/history/personal mix, prepare three pieces of information, then email or return to the current site.
- Deliberate distinction: quiet custom-travel journal, not civic wayfinding and not the Highland Legends heritage-editorial route table.
